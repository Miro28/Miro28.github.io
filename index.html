PROJECT 001 · DIPLOMA

🚀 Mars Rover

A four-wheeled WiFi-controlled rover with a live MJPEG camera stream, tank-style differential steering, and onboard environmental sensing. ESP32-CAM serves the control page in the browser and bridges single-character commands to an Arduino Mega over UART. One battery, one buck converter, two H-bridges, and a lot of GND wires.
